1) Each branch commits to a list of arbitrators
2) You stake bonds in the currency of the parent branch
3) You get paid out in all currencies where the arbitrator thinks you win
4) If you don't escalate to arbitration, you can settle immediately
5) If someone escalates to arbitration, you can't settle until the next window on the branch where it was escalated
6) You need to escalate on any economically-relevant branch
